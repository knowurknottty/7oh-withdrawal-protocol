# 7OH Withdrawal — Quick Reference Card

**Print this. Keep it with you.**

---

## IMMEDIATE ACTIONS (If in active withdrawal)

1. **Hydrate:** Sip electrolytes constantly — don't chug
2. **Loperamide:** 2-4mg for diarrhea (max 16mg/day)
3. **Ibuprofen:** 400-600mg for muscle pain (every 6 hours)
4. **Magnesium:** 400mg glycinate — helps restless legs and anxiety
5. **Vitamin C:** 2000mg now, then 1000mg every 4 hours
6. **Hot shower/bath:** Helps muscle pain and anxiety
7. **Walk:** Even 10 minutes helps — movement reduces symptoms

## EMERGENCY (Go to ER if)

- Can't keep fluids down 24+ hours
- Seizures
- Suicidal thoughts
- Chest pain
- Severe dehydration (dark urine, dizziness)

Tell them: "I'm withdrawing from 7-hydroxymitragynine, an opioid-like substance"

---

## TAPER RULES

1. **Reduce by 10-15% every 7-10 days**
2. **If symptoms spike → HOLD current dose 5-7 days**
3. **Track everything** — dose, symptoms, sleep, mood
4. **Slower is better** — no prize for speed
5. **Never increase dose** during taper

## DO NOT

- ❌ Stop CT (cold turkey) unless medically supervised
- ❌ Drink alcohol during taper
- ❌ Take more than recommended doses
- ❌ Isolate — reach out to someone daily
- ❌ Make major life decisions during acute withdrawal
- ❌ Compare your timeline to others
